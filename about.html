OK. What's to know?

I'm a diverse mixture of many loves and curiosities. These include; Philosophy, Politics, Robotics, Ethics, Symbolic Logic, Rock climbing, Weight Lifting, Animals...you get the picture. It's rare to find something I'm not interested in.

I studied Philosophy and Cognitive Science at university, funding my way through it working in the fitness industry; training, motivating and (hopefully) inspiring my clients.

I loved my degree because it shaped me as a person, gave me an unquenchable thirst for knowledge and taught me how to see through the quagmire of bullshit we find ourselves facing on a daily basis. I love my job because I get to help people achieve awesome things. I also love it because when I go to work, I get to make a positive contribution to my clients' lives.

And there you have it, my ethos for life

In with:

  • Knowledge
  • Awesome things
  • Positive Contributions

Out with:

  • Bullshit

So why do I want to be a Web Developer?

I love creativity. I love structure. I love technology. I want to be a part of something that takes all three, bundles them together and decorates them with a kick-ass bow. I could elaborate on this further but my hope is that as this site develops, my motivation will become clearer through the design decisions I make and the work I post in my blog.
