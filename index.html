Who am I?

Hi there, thanks for stopping by. My name's Jo. I'm 26, I live in Brighton and this, right here, is my first website. Built from scratch. Properly. It may not be much right now, but over the coming months it's going to grow into something amazing.

What am I doing?

I'm currently taking my first, tentative steps on the journey towards web developing. I, like many others (I suspect) had always been fascinated by web design, but assumed I wasn't smart enough and was afraid I'd fail. I studied Cognitive Science at university, but avoided courses like 'Introduction to Programming' for fear of not understanding and looking like a fool. I wish I'd figured it out long ago, but eventually I realised, fear is not an excuse and hey, guess what? It turns out, I am smart enough.
