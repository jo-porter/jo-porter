I'm Jo

I live and work in Brighton, England. It's a friendly little town that likes to pretend it's a city.

I get to do lots of things I love with people I admire and respect. I work at ind.ie with some awesome people, including Aral Balkan and Laura Kalbag.

We have an office dog. As stress-management strategies go, he's a winner.

I used to do lots of work in the fitness industry, where I think I learnt more about human psychology than I did about physiology.

I like to lift weights. I like it a lot. But I'm increasingly learning the value of moderation and sustainability in everything I do.

I occasionally blog about the more interesting things in my life; I am perpetually trying to write more, and realising that time is a commodity in alarmingly short supply.
